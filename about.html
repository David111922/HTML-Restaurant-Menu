Origin Story

How it all began was in a small kitchen in a small house turned Restaurant on Rampart Circle. it all started back in 1965 when Australian Chef David Thompson decided to venture to the United States and bring his culinary expertise to the States. David,famous for his world famous Yam Sun Sen Kung decided to give it a shot. the results were an immediate success. We are proud to say we have 4 locations in the Dade County area.
